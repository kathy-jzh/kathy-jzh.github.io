I was responsible for proposaling the potential development strategies after a detailed research on Ethiopia's current resources, population, location and climate situation.

It is based on the comparative advantage idea proposed by Justin Lifu Lin(Former Chief Economist and Senior Vice President of the World Bank) in his new structural economics theory.
