Projects: The Alien Attack Mini-game

Mini-game: The Alien Attack

Python-pygame
2018

Python - pygame

I learned Python myself with the guide book A Hands-On, Project-based Introduction to Programming, which I love so much and I was greatly inspired by several hands-on projects. So I just tried to write the Alien Attack, and I've simplified the rule of the whole game.

Game Rules:

-Left & Right Button: control rocket's movement; Space Bar: launch a missile
-Maximum five missiles are allowed to coexist in the screen
-Once the missile hits alien, alien and missile will both vanish
-If the rocket has already beats all aliens, then there will soon appear another batch of aliens

I recorded a video to better introducte this mini-game. (I am a terrible player :) haha~)
